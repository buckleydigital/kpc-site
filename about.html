About Kays Pro Cleaning

We're a small, family-owned cleaning business based on the Gold Coast, and we do things differently. As an owner-operated team, we believe trust starts with familiarity, which is why you'll see the same faces at every clean.

Specialising in bond cleans and settlement cleans, we help take the stress out of moving and property preparation. Because we're small and local, we treat your home like we'd treat our own: with meticulous attention to detail, punctuality, and a standard of work we personally stand behind.

You won't get a different contractor each visit or a faceless corporate service. You'll get a consistent team led by the owner, clear communication with the person actually doing the work, and the peace of mind that comes from knowing exactly who's on your property. That's the Kays Pro Cleaning difference.
